Reproducing Evaluation Results

This page describes the scripts and data necessary to reproduce the evaluation for the paper: 5G QoS: Impact of Security Functions on Latency, and the extended paper released in IEEE Communication Magazin: 5G URLLC: A Case Study on Low-Latency Intrusion Prevention.

NOMS 2020 Paper

The paper is presented as part of NOMS 2020.

Sebastian Gallenmüller, Johannes Naab, Iris Adam, Georg Carle, 5G QoS: Impact of Security Functions on Latency, in 2020 IEEE/IFIP Network Operations and Management Symposium (NOMS 2020), Budapest, Hungary, Apr. 2020.
